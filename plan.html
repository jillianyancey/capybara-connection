Getting Here

Location

123 Willow Creek Road
Ashland, OR 97520

Directions

From the North (Medford / I-5 North):
Take I-5 South toward Ashland. Exit at Exit 14 (Ashland / Hwy 66) and turn left onto Hwy 66 West. After 2 miles, turn right onto Willow Creek Road. Follow signs for Capybara Connection.
From the South (Ashland / I-5 North):
 Take I-5 North toward Medford. Exit at Exit 14 (Ashland / Hwy 66) and turn right onto Hwy 66 West. After 2 miles, turn right onto Willow Creek Road. Follow signs for Capybara Connection.

Parking

We offer a spacious, free parking lot just steps from the entrance. Accessible spots are available, and our lot can accommodate cars, vans, and small buses for group visits. Please follow posted signs and staff directions for a smooth arrival.
