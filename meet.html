Meet Them In Person

Come say hello to our capybaras, goats, rabbits, and sheep! Each animal has its own playful personality and story, and there’s no better way to get to know them than up close at Capybara Commons or during a Private Capybara Encounter. From gentle pets to curious sniffs, every visit is a chance to make a new furry friend.
