Capybara Commons

Step into our open farm area to meet our capybaras and their friendly animal neighbors, including goats, sheep, and rabbits, in a relaxed, interactive setting.

Private Encounters

Enjoy a small-group experience with dedicated time to connect, learn, and interact up close with our capybaras. Perfect for a more personal, guided visit.

Sensory Tuesdays

Designed for guests who prefer a quieter, low-stimulation environment, this special session offers a calm and comfortable way to meet our animals.

Our Capybara Crew

Our capybaras are curious, gentle, and full of personality. Each one has their own quirks, favorite snacks, and special traits that make them unique. They love enrichment, relaxing soak time, and meeting new visitors in a calm, positive environment. Get to know the herd and discover what makes these amazing animals so lovable.
